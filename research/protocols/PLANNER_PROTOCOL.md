# Research Planner protocol

Research Planner expands the problem space before selecting one bounded Campaign.
It performs rules/current-state analysis, decision-oriented EDA, baseline/CV
analysis, data-generating-process reasoning, domain and cross-domain research,
independent advisor consultation, anti-anchoring, strategy portfolio management,
and resource-aware convergence.

The Planner may run cheap analyses. It does not own long full-CV/GPU campaigns.
