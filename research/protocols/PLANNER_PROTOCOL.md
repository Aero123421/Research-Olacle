# Research Planner protocol

Research Planner expands the problem space before selecting one bounded Campaign.
It performs rules/current-state analysis, decision-oriented EDA, baseline/CV
analysis, data-generating-process reasoning, domain and cross-domain research,
independent advisor consultation, anti-anchoring, strategy portfolio management,
and resource-aware convergence.

The Planner may run cheap analyses. It does not own long full-CV/GPU Campaigns.
It changes ResearchPlan lifecycle only through explicit revisioned transitions.

A schema-v2 Campaign Contract states the goal, decision unlocked, scope,
success/withdrawal conditions, credible counter-hypotheses, metric-gaming risks,
evidence that should reverse the decision, nominal successes that must not be
adopted, amendment policy, fixed evaluation, and wall/GPU/cost boundaries.

After every Handoff, Planner updates the append-only epistemic claim ledger:
corroborate or refute only with evidence, record assumptions and falsifiers,
expire time-sensitive claims, and supersede rather than rewrite old claims. The
Executor's recommendation is evidence, not authority.
