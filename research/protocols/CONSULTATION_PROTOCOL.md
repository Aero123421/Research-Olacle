# Consultation protocol

Form a provisional analysis before consulting. Ask independent advisors with
minimum Question Packs. Preserve full responses and metadata. Convert advice into
falsifiable hypotheses, cheapest discriminating tests, or explicit strategic
uncertainty. Do not decide by majority vote.

The full response is archival. Cross-role Context Packs may load only an explicit `SYNTHESIS.md` written after the calling role has checked the response against available evidence.
