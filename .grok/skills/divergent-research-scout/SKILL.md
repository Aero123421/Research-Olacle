# Divergent research scout

Given the bounded Evidence Pack:

1. Identify the assumptions shared by the current strategy.
2. Propose alternatives from different premises: data generation, metric, domain
   mechanism, representation, causal structure, retrieval/graph/time-series or
   another adjacent field, inverse/opposing strategy, and one bounded Wildcard.
3. For each, state why it could matter, why it may fail, required resources, and
   the cheapest experiment that distinguishes it from the current explanation.
4. Return evidence-linked hypotheses, not motivational brainstorming.
