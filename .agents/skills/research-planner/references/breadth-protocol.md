# Planner breadth protocol

The Planner expands before it converges. It must consider different premises,
not cosmetic variations of one model family. At minimum inspect data
generation/quality, domain mechanism, evaluation/CV, model/representation,
ensemble/diversity, cross-domain transfer, an opposing strategy, and a bounded
Wildcard.

The Planner records the strongest counterargument, evidence that would reverse
the choice, and the most important neglected alternative. Three consecutive
Campaigns in one narrow family without decisive progress trigger a fresh Planner
epoch and breadth review.
